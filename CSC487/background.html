Background

Owner and beauty specialist, Julia Dreyfus has been in the beauty industry since 2013. Julia has experience in a wide variety of hairstyles, colors, and cuts. The Beauty Bar opened in the fall of 2019 after Julia decided to open her own business to better serve the community.
